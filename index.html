Related Work Experience

CIRAS Summer Internship

During the summer of 2023, I held an internship with ISU's Center for Industrial Research and Service. Their goal is to help small to medium sized businesses grow by engaging, educating, and embedding.
My role in my internship was varied. First, I conducted research for a potential “management systems” service that CIRAS could provide to customers. Next, I collaborated on a project with a client and other interns to help them identify system requirements for a potential project. Third, I had an opportunity to drive to a business and take a tour to understand their processes with the goal of assisting them with processes mapping. Last, me and the other interns had the privilege of taking extensive business tours to learn more about how companies around Iowa operate.
I accepted this internship with the goal of improving the management side of my management info systems degree. Finishing the internship, I felt more well balanced with newfound skills in business to business communication and an overall understanding of how businesses operate.

Level1 Freelance Work

In May 2023, I was hired by an esports production company named Level1 to work on an event they needed extra hands with. The event was called Battle Of BC 5, a major tournament for a game called Super Smash Bros. My job was to create the graphics for the livestream. The graphics are powered off a “web browser source” in their livestream software, so all the graphics were written using Typescript, Lit, and CSS.
I was provided graphics concepts created by a designer, but I still had to replicate the designs in HTML and CSS while plugging in reactive data (eg. scoreboard data, player names, etc.) to a dashboard app connected to a web server. I created about 13 different graphics for the event's stream in the end.

maps.iplabs.ink

maps.iplabs.ink is a web tool I've created to help people in the competitive Splatoon scene. The game has 4 game maps and dozens of stages you can play on. The goal of this site is to automate the creation of map lists for tournament play. The challenge was to create map lists that contain an equal amount of maps and modes while seeming varied.

IPL Media Creator

To help boost social media engagement for our esport events, I've created a discord bot that can quickly turn a twitch clip into an mp4 file with our logos and link to view added to the bottom of clips automatically. With this tool, we can post clips from our livestream to our social media pages in about one minute. The tool can also create graphics automatically based on info the user provides.
